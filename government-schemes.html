Key State & Central Schemes

  • Annadata Sukhibhava: ₹20,000/year to land-owning farmers (<5 acres) in Andhra Pradesh.
  • Urea Reduction Incentive: ₹800 per urea bag for reducing urea usage.
  • PM-KISAN: ₹6,000 annually to eligible small and marginal farmers.
  • National Food Security Mission (NFSM): Subsidy on quality seeds and fertilizers for rice, pulses, and coarse cereals.
  • Rastriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY): Financial support for agricultural development and infrastructure.
  • National Mission on Oilseeds & Oil Palm (NMOOPS): Subsidies for oilseed cultivation and oil palm plantations.
  • Seed Supply on Subsidy: Quality seeds provided at subsidized rates to ensure timely sowing.
